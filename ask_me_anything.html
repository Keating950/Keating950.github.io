Ask me Anything
 

"Ask me Anything: Mining through the 10 year history of /r/McGill" is a feature-length data journalism project that I produced with my colleague Kyle Dewsnap in the winter of 2019—2020. We scraped every post in the subreddit's history, totalling over 30,000, from Reddit's official API and enriched them with data from pushshift.io. I then applied various natural language processing techniques in Python, such as k-means clustering and sentiment analysis; the notebook below demonstrates some examples of my work.
